About Your Treatment

Neurotoxin (such as Botox, Dysport, or Xeomin) is an FDA-approved injectable that temporarily relaxes targeted muscles to smooth lines and wrinkles. Results typically last 3–4 months and vary by individual.

Common side effects (temporary):

  • Mild bruising or swelling at injection sites
  • Slight headache or tenderness

Rare side effects (temporary):

  • Eyelid or brow drooping
  • Minor asymmetry

These are uncommon and typically resolve on their own within a few weeks.

This treatment is not appropriate if you:

  • Are pregnant or nursing
  • Have a neuromuscular disorder (e.g., myasthenia gravis)
  • Have a known allergy to botulinum toxin or its ingredients
