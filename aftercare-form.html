Neurotoxin Aftercare Instructions

Thank you for your visit. Follow these simple guidelines
to get the best results from your treatment.

Visit Details
Your Aftercare Guide

First 4 Hours

  • Stay upright — avoid lying down or bending over
  • Gently exercise treated muscles (frown, raise your brows, squint)
  • Do not touch, rub, or massage the injection sites
  • Skip strenuous exercise or heavy lifting

First 24 Hours

  • Avoid alcohol
  • Skip facials, chemical peels, and microdermabrasion
  • No hot tubs, saunas, or steam rooms
  • Sleep on your back if possible

First 2 Weeks

  • Results begin appearing in 3–5 days, with full effect at 14 days
  • Contact us if you notice significant asymmetry after day 14
  • A complimentary touch-up is available within 2 weeks if needed

When to Contact Us Right Away

These reactions are rare, but please call us immediately if you experience:

  • Severe or persistent headache
  • Vision changes or difficulty opening your eyes
  • Difficulty swallowing or breathing

These are very uncommon. We include them so you know what to look for.
