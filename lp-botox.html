COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Results typically last 3 to 4 months, though some clients experience results for up to 6 months with regular treatments. Over time, many clients find they need fewer units as the muscles become trained to relax. We recommend scheduling maintenance appointments to keep your results looking their best.
Most clients describe the sensation as a small pinch that lasts only a second. We use ultra-fine needles and can apply topical numbing cream beforehand for maximum comfort. Many clients are surprised at how quick and painless the process is — treatments typically take just 10 to 20 minutes.
You may begin to notice subtle changes within 24 to 48 hours, with full results visible in 10 to 14 days. We schedule a complimentary follow-up at the two-week mark to assess your results and make any adjustments if needed to ensure you are completely satisfied.
Pricing varies based on the number of units needed and the areas being treated. We provide transparent pricing during your complimentary consultation after assessing your specific goals and anatomy. We believe in honest, upfront pricing with no hidden fees or surprise charges.
There is virtually no downtime. You can return to most daily activities immediately after your treatment. We recommend avoiding strenuous exercise, lying flat, and rubbing the treated areas for about 4 hours post-treatment. Minor redness or small bumps at the injection sites typically resolve within 15 to 30 minutes.
Most healthy adults over the age of 18 are good candidates for Botox. It is not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or for individuals with certain neuromuscular conditions. During your complimentary consultation, we will review your medical history, discuss your goals, and determine the best treatment plan for you.
