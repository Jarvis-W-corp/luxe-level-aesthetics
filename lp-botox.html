COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Botox and Daxify are neurotoxins that relax the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles like forehead lines, frown lines, and crow's feet. Daxify is a newer neurotoxin that may last longer for some patients. Dermal fillers, on the other hand, restore lost volume and sculpt facial contours — they are used for lips, cheeks, jawline, under-eyes, and nasolabial folds. Many clients benefit from a combination of both for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.
Botox typically lasts 3 to 4 months, while Daxify may last up to 6 months for some clients. Dermal filler results vary by product and treatment area: lip fillers generally last 6 to 12 months, cheek and jawline fillers can last 12 to 18 months, and Sculptra results may last up to 2 years. We will recommend a personalized maintenance schedule during your consultation.
Neurotoxin injections feel like a small pinch and take just 10 to 15 minutes. For dermal fillers, we apply topical numbing cream and many fillers contain built-in lidocaine for added comfort. For lip treatments, a dental block can be used to minimize sensation. Most clients are surprised at how comfortable the experience is.
Dermal filler results are visible immediately, though final results settle in over 1 to 2 weeks as any swelling subsides. Botox and Daxify begin to take effect within 24 to 48 hours, with full results visible in 10 to 14 days. We schedule a complimentary follow-up at the two-week mark to assess your results and make any adjustments if needed.
Pricing varies based on the type of injectable, the number of units or syringes needed, and the areas being treated. We provide transparent pricing during your complimentary consultation after assessing your specific goals and anatomy. We believe in honest, upfront pricing with no hidden fees or surprise charges.
Most healthy adults over the age of 18 are good candidates for injectable treatments. Neurotoxins and fillers are not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding. During your complimentary consultation, we will review your medical history, discuss your goals, and determine whether neurotoxins, fillers, or a combination will deliver the best results for you.
