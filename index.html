We offer complimentary consultations for all of our services. During your consultation, Brittany will assess your concerns, discuss your goals, review your medical history, and recommend a personalized treatment plan. There is absolutely no obligation or pressure.
Your first visit is a relaxed, no-pressure consultation. You will tour our studio, meet Brittany, discuss your concerns and goals, and receive a recommended treatment plan with transparent pricing. Many clients choose to begin treatment the same day, but there is never any obligation to do so.
Yes, we offer flexible payment options. We accept all major credit cards and can discuss financing options during your consultation. We believe that looking and feeling your best should be accessible, and we work with each client to find a comfortable arrangement.
