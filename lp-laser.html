COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Most clients describe the sensation as a warm snapping feeling, similar to a rubber band lightly flicking the skin. We use integrated cooling technology and topical numbing cream to keep you comfortable throughout the session. Pain tolerance varies, but the vast majority of our clients find the treatments very manageable.
The number of sessions depends on the treatment type and your specific concerns. Laser hair removal typically requires 6 to 8 sessions, while skin rejuvenation and pigmentation treatments may show significant improvement in 3 to 5 sessions. During your complimentary consultation, we will create a personalized treatment plan with a clear timeline for your expected results.
Downtime varies by treatment type. Non-ablative treatments like IPL and skin tightening typically have little to no downtime — you may experience mild redness for a few hours. Ablative resurfacing treatments may involve 3 to 7 days of redness and peeling as your skin heals and reveals fresh, rejuvenated layers underneath. We provide detailed aftercare instructions tailored to your specific treatment.
Laser treatments are remarkably versatile. We treat sun damage and age spots, fine lines and wrinkles, acne scars, uneven skin tone and texture, rosacea and facial redness, vascular lesions and broken capillaries, unwanted hair, and skin laxity. During your consultation, we will evaluate your concerns and recommend the ideal laser modality for your goals.
We recommend avoiding sun exposure and tanning for at least two weeks before your treatment. Discontinue retinoids and exfoliating acids 5 to 7 days prior. Arrive with clean skin free of makeup, lotions, and sunscreen. Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements if possible. We will provide you with a complete preparation guide during your consultation.
Yes. With the right technology and expertise, laser treatments can be performed safely and effectively on all skin types, including darker skin tones. We use laser systems with specific wavelengths and adjustable parameters designed for higher Fitzpatrick skin types. Our experienced technician carefully calibrates each treatment to minimize any risk of hyperpigmentation while delivering excellent results.
